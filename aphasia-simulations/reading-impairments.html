Reading Impairments

Some people with aphasia have difficulty processing the written words that they see.

Reading impairments can significantly impact a person's ability to understand written text, making everyday tasks challenging. These exercises aim to simulate some of the difficulties faced by individuals with reading impairments due to aphasia.
