Writing Impairments Communication Tips

Effective communication with individuals experiencing writing impairments due to aphasia requires patience, understanding, and alternative strategies. Here are some helpful tips:

  • Encourage the person to write the first letter, or draw slots for how many letters there are in the word.
  • Encourage the person to speak, draw, or gesture to communicate the message they are trying to get across.
  • Be patient and allow extra time for the person to express themselves through writing.
  • Offer multiple-choice options or a list of words to choose from if the person is struggling to write a specific word.
  • Consider using assistive technology or apps designed to help with writing and communication for individuals with aphasia.
