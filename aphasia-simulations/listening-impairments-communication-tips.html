Listening Impairments Communication Tips

Key Tips for Communicating with People with Listening Impairments:

  • Speak slowly and clearly, but maintain a natural tone of voice.
  • Use simple, concise sentences and avoid complex language.
  • Repeat key information and rephrase if necessary.
  • Use gestures, facial expressions, and visual aids to support your communication.
  • Be patient and allow extra time for the person to process information.
  • Minimize background noise and distractions during conversations.
  • Encourage and validate their efforts to communicate.
